Vulnerable By Design

podcast · listening · vulnerability
October 2021–ongoing
website: Vulnerable By Design ⇗

Vulnerable By Design is a podcast about being vulnerable in today’s techno-societies. Topics covered so far include: molotov cocktails, the ‘Like’-button, the potential collapse of the micro-targeted ad industry, and … normality? Yes, normality.

This podcast started as part of the Engaging Vulnerability programme at Uppsala University.
